### How task managment app must work ###
> A task could have one or more child tasks.
> And a child task could have one or more granchild tasks.
> Tasks could be organized by a group.
> A group of tasks represents a task flow for an especific work to do,
like homeworks, personal projects, personal plans. etc.
> A task must be controlled, using data like periodicity,
what day to do a work (ex: Monday, Wednesday and Friday i would go to
the gym), how many day at week, etc. Must define
start time and end time.
